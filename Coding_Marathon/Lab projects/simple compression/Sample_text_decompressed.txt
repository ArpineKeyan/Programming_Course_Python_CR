Armenians are an ethnic group native to the Armenian highlands of Western Asia. constitute main population Armenia and de facto independent Artsakh. There is a wide-ranging diaspora around five million people full or partial ancestry living outside modern Armenia. The largest populations today exist in Russia, United States, France, Georgia, Iran, Germany, Ukraine, Lebanon, Brazil, Syria. With exceptions Iran former Soviet states, present-day was formed mainly as result genocide. Indo-European language. It has two mutually intelligible spoken written forms: Eastern Armenian, Armenia, Artsakh, republics; used historical and, after genocide, primarily diasporan communities. unique alphabet invented 405 AD by Mesrop Mashtots. Most adhere Apostolic Church, non-Chalcedonian Christian church, which also world's oldest national church. Christianity began spread soon Jesus' death, due efforts his apostles, St. Thaddeus Bartholomew. In early 4th century, Kingdom became first state adopt religion.
